Our Institution

143 years of shaping Naushahro Feroze's future

A purely academic institution, teaching Class I through Class X under the Sindh curriculum — with no parallel religious wing. Every classroom here exists for one purpose: building capable, confident students.

Our History

Founded in 1883 by Sir Sayed Allahnado Shah, this institution stands among the oldest seats of learning in Naushahro Feroze. For over 140 years it has carried forward a single mission: opening the door of education to every child in this district.

Recognition

Registered with SEMIS code 416040596 and affiliated with the Board of Intermediate & Secondary Education (BISE), Shaheed Benazir Abad, for Matriculation examinations.

Our Mission

Every student who walks through these gates leaves carrying more than a report card — they carry discipline, curiosity and the confidence to shape their own future. We exist to turn potential into achievement, one classroom, one child at a time.

Admissions 2026–27

How to Enroll Your Child

Admissions are open for Class I through Class IX (Class X only for students continuing from Class IX at this campus).

Required Documents

  • Original B-Form / CNIC of parent or guardian
  • Birth certificate (issued by Union Council)
  • Previous school leaving certificate (if applicable)
  • 2 passport-size photographs
  • Immunization / vaccination card (Primary section)

Important Dates

Admission Form Distribution1 – 20 Aug 2026
Entry Test (Class VI & above)25 Aug 2026
Merit List Display29 Aug 2026
Fee Submission Deadline05 Sep 2026
Classes Commence08 Sep 2026
Admission forms are available free of cost from the school office, Naushahro Feroze, Monday–Saturday, 8:00 AM–1:00 PM. No online submission at this time — please visit the campus in person with original documents.
